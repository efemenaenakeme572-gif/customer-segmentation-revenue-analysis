Overview
This project presents an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard developed in Power BI to analyse customer segmentation, revenue distribution, and performance trends.
The objective was to transform raw transactional data into a structured analytical model that provides clear, executive-level visibility of revenue drivers and customer concentration patterns.


Business Context:
Organisations require structured reporting to understand:
Which customer segments generate the most revenue
How revenue trends evolve over time
The distribution of revenue across customers
The level of dependency on top-performing customers
This dashboard simulates a real-world reporting scenario designed to support data-driven strategic decision-making.


Key Objectives:
Provide a consolidated view of total revenue, orders, and quantity sold;
Analyse monthly revenue trends to identify seasonality and growth patterns;
Segment customers by revenue contribution;
Assess revenue concentration risk using Top 10 customer analysis;
Deliver a clean, executive-ready reporting layout.


Technical Approach:
Cleaned and transformed data using Power Query
Designed a structured data model to support accurate aggregation
Developed DAX measures including:
Total Revenue
Total Orders
Total Quantity
Top 10 Revenue % (concentration analysis)
Applied Top N filtering for customer ranking
Implemented professional dashboard design principles focusing on clarity, spacing, and hierarchy


Key Insights Delivered:
Revenue is diversified across a wide customer base
The Top 10 customers contribute approximately 2.7% of total revenue, indicating low concentration risk
Clear segmentation visibility across Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office segments
Monthly trend analysis highlights performance fluctuations across the year


Tools & Technologies:
Power BI Desktop;
Power Query;
DAX;
Data Modelling.
